Line Following Bot

The objective is to make your own line following robot which can complete custom tracks accurately in the shortest amount of time.
The autonomous robot will begin it's position from an initial marked position in a dark isolated room. It will follow the line strip beneath it and would cover the track. The robot has to lead it's path by sensing the line. The objective is to cover the entire track in least possible time with sufficient accuracy.

Rounds :

Round 1

  • - There will be a single track.
  • - There is no such limit on the time of completion of the bot run however the team which completes the run in minimum time qualifies for the next round.
  • - Maximum of 8 hand touches (but with time penalty) and one hand pick (with extra time penalty) is allowed throughout the round in order to adjust its position if it is heading wrong.
  • - There will be one checkpoint and if it gets skipped, additional penalty will be charged accordingly.
  • - The team cannot make any programming changes in the course once the bot starts its run.
  • - Calibration time will be provided before the competition.

Round 2

  • - Track complexity increases this time.
  • - Each team will have maximum of 5 minutes to complete the track.
  • - Maximum of 6 hand touches (but with penalty) and 2 hand picks (with extra penalty) are allowed throughout the round in order to adjust its position if it is heading wrong.
  • - There will be four checkpoints and maximum two can be skipped but penalty will be charged accordingly.
  • - The team cannot make any programming changes in the course once the bot starts its run.

Rules :

  • - Decision of chief organizers and event heads is final and every participant has to accept it.
  • - Round 1 is completing the track efficiently and in less time.
  • - Clearing round 1 in an average minimum stipulated time ensures qualification to the next round.
  • - Each team can have maximum of 4 members.
  • - Each team should have unique participants i.e. it cannot have any common member.
  • - Cross college teams are allowed.
  • - The Right spirit of participation and discipline (during the competition) is expected from the participants.

Event :

  • - There will be a line present in the arena. This line can be white on black background or black on white background.
  • - The thickness of line will not be the same.
  • - The track will have breaks in it i.e. gaps.
  • - In case the robot is not able to cross the gap they can reposition it but marks will be deducted for it.

Restart & Timeout :

  • - A maximum of 1 Timeouts of 5 Minutes each may be taken in the second round only.
  • - A penalty will be rewarded for each Timeout taken by the team and the robots will start from the start zone.
  • - The participants can have a maximum of 2 restarts for rounds. A penalty will be imposed for each restart.

Carnage

This is an event with an aim to construct an autonomous bot which can successfully pass through each of the hurdle in a specified order. The teams will compete on completing the course in the fastest time possible and rack up the most points which are awarded on each successful hurdle pass.

Phases :

  • Round 1 : Qualification on the basis of time. The competition is based upon time trail system. This round will act as qualifying round for each team. The top team to qualifying makes it on the basis of time trials.
  • Round 2 : Elimination on the basis of perfection and time. This will act as the final round. All about final round will be notified on the final day.

Rules :

  • - Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.
  • - Each team should have unique robot to participate in the event.
  • - Cross college teams are allowed
  • - The Right spirit of participation and discipline (during the competition) is expected from the participants.
  • - The decision of the EVENT heads of CARNAGE will be final and binding on all the participants without any exceptions.
  • - The event will be totally time based and additional time penalties will be declared before the event starts.
  • - It is the participant’s responsibility to know about all the rules and then start to perform.
  • - Scores of any team will not be declared and will be kept confidential.

Bot Specifications :

  • - The maximum dimension of the robot can be 30 x 30 cm (l x b).

It is the time to concentrate on the hacking and slashing of the robots. So, make your bots ready and leave them at the arena to feel the game. The game of SOCCER!!
